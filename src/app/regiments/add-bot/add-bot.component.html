Invite to Discord

Regiment Owner Requirements

  • Get started by inviting Regimental Assistant to your Discord server.
  • Ensure the regiment owner has admin permissions on your Discord server.
  • Sync your Discord and WoRTool accounts to confirm your ownership.

Step-by-Step

  • Sync Discord with your WoRTool account in Settings > Profile & Accounts to enable the ability to own a regiment.
  • Invite the Regimental Assistant to your Discord server using the "Invite to Discord" button.
  • Run the command "wor!setup <usa/csa>" in an applicable bot-spam channel.
  • Cheers your regiment is now on WoRTool! You will have to re-login to WoRTool to access your regiment dashboard.
