Requirements

  • Must be a registered user with a synced Discord account.
  • Must invite the Regimental Assistant bot to your Discord server.
  • Must have Administrative privileges on your Discord Server.

Step-by-Step

  • Sync Discord in Settings > Profile & Accounts to enable ability to own a regiment.
  • Invite the Regimental Assistant to your Discord server using the "Invite to Discord" button.
  • Run the command "pa!setup <usa/csa>" in an applicable bot-spam channel.

    *You must have admin privileges and being using the same Discord account that is synced to your user account.

  • Your Regiment has been added! Users can now navigate to Settings > Profile & Accounts and join your regiment.
